What We Do

We focus on the base fabric that matters before colour, polish, and finishing begin.

Built for mills, traders, and saree processors

Our role is clear: weave patterned polyester saree grey material with dependable structure and weight so the next stage of processing starts on the right foundation.

What makes this unit different

  • Focused only on grey saree material, not ready coloured sarees.
  • Machine-led weaving suited for repeatable pattern development.
  • Surat-based manufacturing aligned with textile market timelines.
  • Clean presentation of designs, weights, and gallery references.
